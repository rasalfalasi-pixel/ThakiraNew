# Thakira (Memory)

**Thakira** is a digital storytelling platform designed to preserve and celebrate cultural heritage through immersive technology. By combining interactive gameplay with Virtual Reality (VR) environments, the project allows users to travel through various historical eras and experience heritage firsthand.

## Overview

The name **Thakira** (Arabic for **Memory**) reflects the core mission of the project: ensuring that historical narratives and cultural identities are not forgotten in the digital age. The platform serves as a bridge between the past and the present, making history accessible and engaging for a modern audience.

## Key Features

* **Immersive VR Experience:** High fidelity environments that transport users to specific historical periods.
* **Interactive Storytelling:** A narrative driven approach where user choices and actions influence the progression of the story.
* **Heritage Preservation:** Accurate digital reconstructions of historical sites, artifacts, and traditions.
* **Educational Gameplay:** Mechanics designed to inform users about history while maintaining an engaging gaming experience.

## Technical Stack

* **Engine:** Unity (or Unreal Engine, depending on your build)
* **Platform:** VR Headsets (e.g., Meta Quest) and Desktop
* **Design Tools:** High fidelity mockups and detailed UI/UX workflows

## Project Structure

The repository contains the following components:
* **Source Code:** Core logic for the interactive elements and VR integration.
* **Documentation:** Use case diagrams, class diagrams, and sequence diagrams detailing the system architecture.
* **Assets:** Design prompts and assets related to various historical eras.
